The first page I found is a website with only 2 pages: a home page and a prices page. The graphics and font are fun, but the layout of the page isn't very visually pleasing or engaging and only 3 pictures of pets are provided. I would include more pet pictures, or perhaps a picture of the establishment to help people identify it when they arrive.

Another website I found doesn't offer much on the front page, simply prompting for a phone number. I'm not a fan of this because I don't like giving out my phone number to just any website, so I would create a more open website that allows viewers to sus out whether they actually want the groomer's services before they have to offer up any personal information.

Yet another website I found only has one low-quality picture of a poodle on the homepage. The rest of the graphics they use don't really say "dog groomer". Some of the important text on the page is very small compared to all the other elements on the page. I would improve this page by giving it a more fun color pallete and graphics, higher quality pictures of pets, and increase the font size for easier readability, especially for those who can't see very well.

The elements I liked about all these pages were the inclusion of pet pictures, easy to find navigation, and addresses or contact info on the first page.
